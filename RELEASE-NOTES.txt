Version 3.0.0
=============
As of version 3, we will publish and maintain two versions of klojang-check. One version will be compiled against JDK 17
while the other will be allowed to move along with the JDK release cadence. The version strings will reflect this. So
we now have version 3.0.0-jdk17 and version 3.0.0-jdk21 of klojang-check.
